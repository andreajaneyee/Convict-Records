Moreton Bay Convict Records

1824 - 1839



Students and researchers can perform filtered searches and generate graphs based on open datasets from the Queensland State Archives. These datasets are from the Chronological Register of Convicts at Moreton Bay, 1824-1839. The indexes and their transcriptions were completed by the State Library of Queensland and Queensland State Archives volunteers.

For more information, visit the Queensland State Archives website.
